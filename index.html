Understanding the Deaf Community

The deaf community has a rich culture and language of its own. Sign languages are complete natural languages with their own grammar and lexicon.

Breaking Barriers

Only about 30% of spoken English can be understood through lip reading. Sign language provides complete access to communication for deaf individuals.

Technology for Inclusion

Modern technology can help bridge the communication gap between deaf and hearing communities through innovative solutions like SignSync.
